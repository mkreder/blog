<p><!--:en-->Last week I attended <a href="http://cislargentina.org/">CISL</a>. The event was good, several people came to our stand to talk about Fedora and ARM (I brought a HummingBoard and a Raspberry Pi).<br>I had the pleasure of meeting with Jon "maddog" Hall, director of Linux International. We took a picture together and then he told me about the community effort of translating assembler code into C or ASM compatible with ARMv8+ (aarch64).</p>

<p>La semana pasada estuve en la <a href="http://cislargentina.org/">CISL</a>. El evento estuvo bueno, bastante gente se acerco a nuestro stand a hablar sobre Fedora y ARM .
